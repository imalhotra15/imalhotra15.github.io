Autonomous Excavator

University at Buffalo (Distributed Robotics and Networked Embedded Systems Lab) in collaboration with MOOG Construction

Introduction

This project focuses on building an autonomous excavator to carry out multi-agent collaborative tasks. This project is being carried out as a collaboration between DRONES Lab @ UB and MOOG Construction. The excavator is equipped with a variety of sensors and actuators to enable autonomous operation. The project aims to develop a robust and reliable system for autonomous excavation tasks.
