About Me

Hello! I’m currently pursuing a Master of Science in Robotics at the University at Buffalo, bringing hands-on experience from internships and research roles. My recent work with Danfoss and in our university’s research lab has deepened my expertise in autonomous systems, from advanced simulations to real-time robotics applications. I specialize in integrating complex systems for both on-road and off-highway projects, leveraging technologies like path planning, visual odometry, and deep learning. My background also includes leading data science initiatives in the electric vehicles space, where I optimized performance and managed engineering projects. This unique intersection of robotics and data science fuels my passion for developing robust autonomous solutions. With a strong foundation in Python, ROS, PyTorch, and a deep understanding of perception and planning algorithms, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to your innovative team and drive advancements in autonomous systems. Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how my skills align with your team's goals.
